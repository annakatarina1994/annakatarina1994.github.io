About Me

I took time off of school to work and reflect. I learned a lot about myself and I decided I wanted to go into a technical field as I did not really feel fulfilled in the financial sector, but also did not feel that Social Work was the right fit for me. I began a second Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering. There I joined the Supremileage club at the Center for Automotive Research, where my love for cars was quickly reignited and I wound up being voted in as President.

However, I found myself more interested in artificial intelligence and programming in cars. This led me to the We Can Code IT coding bootcamp. I wanted to get a foundation on coding and start my path towards learning about AI. Eventually, I’d like to have a hand in the AI involved with autonomous vehicles.
